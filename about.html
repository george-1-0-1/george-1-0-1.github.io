Who I Am

I'm George David, a robotics engineer and AI/ML developer with a keen interest in autonomous systems and intelligent multi-agent coordination. Currently pursuing my M.Sc in Robotics at the University of Bristol, I bring hands-on experience in designing and implementing robotic systems that tackle real-world challenges.

My journey in technology spans from traditional embedded systems programming to cutting-edge machine learning and large language models. I'm particularly fascinated by the intersection of robotics and artificial intelligence, where I've contributed to research in swarm robotics, distributed algorithms, and secure multi-agent communication.

With a collaborative mindset and strong leadership background, I've managed diverse teams, coordinated international robotics competitions, and presented research at top conferences. My goal is to create systems that are not just intelligent, but also resilient, ethical, and beneficial to society.
